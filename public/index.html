Enrollment

How exactly do I enrol with you guys?

The first step is contacting us from the section below and letting us know how we can help you. After that, one of our mentors will get back to you for a session, where you'll be able to judge their teaching as well as have an informal chat outlining what you expect and a rough plan for the road ahead. Once that's done, then we jump straight into classes!

Availability

Am I guaranteed a spot if I express interest?

We try to accommodate a healthy number of students, however, we're committed to providing the best quality of teaching to the students under our care ‚Äì zero compromises. Since GeorgeTheTutor is a fledgling project at the moment, we unfortunately can't guarantee a spot to every student who expresses interest, due to our limited capacity.

However, this shouldn't dissuade you from reaching out to us! Even if we can't offer you a direct mentoring spot, you'll be given access to GeorgeTheTutor servers and forums where you can discuss subject matter with other students, as well as our team mentors' contact information so you can reach out any time!

Pricing

How are your classes priced?

One of our main objectives is to provide quality education at a reasonable price. What matters for us is what and how you're learning, much more than what you pay. We do not have any fixed fees. Rather, we allow our students to pay as much as they wish to.

Class Size

How many students are present in a single class?

We're committed to ensuring that each student has a personalised, effective and enriching education. Therefore, our classes are either one-on-one, or restricted to 2-3 students maximum.
